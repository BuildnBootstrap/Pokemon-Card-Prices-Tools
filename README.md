## Price Guides & Trackers

- [TCGplayer Price Guides](https://www.tcgplayer.com/categories/trading-and-collectible-card-games/pokemon/price-guides) - Per-set price guides for singles and sealed product, showing Market Price (recent sales) and Listed Median (current listings).
- [Cardmarket](https://www.cardmarket.com/en/Pokemon) - Europe's largest Pokémon marketplace, with per-card trend and average sale prices in EUR from its own listings and completed sales.
- [PokePriceTracker](https://www.pokemonpricetracker.com/) - Price tracker aggregating TCGplayer, eBay, and Cardmarket data for raw and graded cards, with historical charts and a developer API.
- [PokeDATA](https://www.pokedata.io/) - Price tracking and valuation for singles and sealed product, with charts, alerts, sold-frequency data, and portfolios across English, Japanese, and Chinese editions.
- [Pokémon Wizard](https://www.pokemonwizard.com/) - Hourly-updated card and sealed pricing (from TCGplayer) with free collection tracking, price alerts, and market-trend stats.
- [Card Hedge](https://ai.cardhedger.com/price-guide/category/pokemon) - Trading-card price guide with a Pokémon section tracking real-time values, price history, and sales data.
- [TCG Price Tracker](https://tcgpricetracker.com/) - Tracks card and sealed prices across Cardmarket, TCGplayer, and eBay, with cross-marketplace arbitrage alerts and CSV/webhook export.

## Market Data & Indices

- [TCGscreener](https://tcgscreener.com/pokemon) - Free analytics with screening for every price, variable, and artist. Gives every card and set a modeled dollar marketcap, with daily price tracking, a Pokémon price index, and PSA population data plus an is-it-worth-grading tool.
- [PriceCharting](https://www.pricecharting.com/category/pokemon-cards) - Extensive Price history across graded and loose cards.
- [Card Ladder](https://www.cardladder.com/) - Sales-history and market-index platform aggregating public card sales (eBay, Goldin, Heritage, and more) into price guides and indexes.
- [PokeInvesting](https://pokeinvesting.com/) - Portfolio and price-tracking platform for English and Japanese cards, with daily movers, a PSA grading-ROI calculator, and inventory bookkeeping.
- [Market Movers](https://www.marketmoversapp.com/) - Pricing and index app (sports-card-first) with a dedicated Pokémon price guide, aggregating graded and raw sales comps from eBay, Goldin, Heritage, and PSA.

## Collection & Portfolio Tracking

- [TCG Collector](https://www.tcgcollector.com/) - Collection tracker with a complete international and Japanese set database, per-variant tracking, custom lists, and filters.
- [Pokéllector](https://www.pokellector.com/) - Card database and collection tracker for browsing by set and cataloging owned/wanted cards, with mobile apps.
- [pkmn.gg](https://www.pkmn.gg/) - Collection tracker and deck builder with set/variant completion tracking and TCGplayer-sourced same-day collection value.
- [Collectr](https://www.getcollectr.com/) - Portfolio app that catalogs and values raw, graded, and sealed items across 25+ TCGs, with AI scanning and real-time pricing.
- [CollX](https://www.collx.app/) - Mobile app that scans and identifies cards against a 20M+ database, showing recent-transaction values and portfolio worth.
- [Ludex](https://www.ludex.com/) - AI image-recognition app to scan, identify, value, and organize cards, with sales-based pricing and eBay listing tools.
- [Dex](https://dextcg.com/) - Independent (unofficial) iOS/Android/web app to scan cards, track collections, check values, and build decks.
- [My TCG Collection](https://mytcgcollection.com/) - Free web collection tracker with a booster-pack simulator and 30/60/90-day value data across English and Japanese sets.

## Grading & Population

- [PSA Population Report](https://www.psacard.com/pop) - PSA's official free population report showing graded counts per grade, searchable by set and card, updated daily.
- [GemRate](https://www.gemrate.com/) - Aggregates daily population reports from PSA, Beckett, SGC, and CGC into a single Universal Pop Report and tracks grading trends.
- [CGC Cards Population Report](https://www.cgccards.com/population-report/tcg/) - CGC's official TCG population report, browsable by game, era, and set.
- [Beckett (BGS) Population Report](https://www.beckett.com/grading/pop-report) - Beckett's free population report broken down by grade across sports and TCG.
- [TAG Grading Pop Report](https://my.taggrading.com/pop-report) - Searchable population report from AI-assisted grader TAG, with per-card counts, rankings, and chronology.
- [SGC Population Report](https://gosgc.com/pop-report) - SGC's free population report for per-grade certified counts.

## Databases & APIs

- [Pokémon TCG API](https://pokemontcg.io/) - REST API serving set/card data, images, and bundled TCGplayer/Cardmarket prices as JSON.
- [Scrydex](https://scrydex.com/) - Commercial multi-TCG developer API with card metadata, images, and raw plus graded pricing with history.
- [TCGdex](https://tcgdex.dev/) - Free, open-source multilingual Pokémon TCG API over REST and GraphQL, with SDKs and 10+ languages.
- [Limitless TCG Card Database](https://limitlesstcg.com/cards) - Multi-language card database with advanced search and TCGplayer/Cardmarket prices.
- [PkmnCards](https://pkmncards.com/) - Searchable card database and high-res image repository with advanced query-syntax search.
- [PokéWallet API](https://www.pokewallet.io/) - Developer API and card database for Pokémon and One Piece, with US/EU price data via REST/GraphQL.
- [Serebii Cardex](https://www.serebii.net/card/dex/) - Encyclopedic card database indexing every Pokémon's card appearances, plus Trainers, Energy, artists, and attacks.
- [Bulbapedia](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_Trading_Card_Game) - Community-run encyclopedia documenting every expansion, card, set, and mechanic.

## Communities

- [r/PokemonTCG](https://www.reddit.com/r/PokemonTCG/) - The main Pokémon TCG subreddit for news, pulls, deck help, and market discussion.
- [Elite Fourum](https://www.elitefourum.com/) - Forum for serious Pokémon TCG collectors, with curated resource and reference threads.

## Deck Building & Play

- [Pokémon TCG Live](https://tcg.pokemon.com/en-us/tcgl/) - Official free-to-play digital Pokémon TCG with an in-app deck builder and online play.
- [Limitless Deck Builder](https://my.limitlesstcg.com/) - Web deck builder with advanced search, prices, image exports, and playtesting.
- [PokemonCard](https://pokemoncard.io/) - Card database and online deck builder with user-submitted decks, meta lists, and deck pricing.
- [CardForge](https://www.cardforgeapp.gg/) - Deck builder with tournament meta data, deck-health grading, and one-click export to Pokémon TCG Live.
